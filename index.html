01 / SHELL AROUND Z-AXIS

Shell method.

V = ∫ 2π · x · f(x) · dx

Wraps the solid in nested cylindrical shells. Each shell's circumference times its height times its thickness equals its volume — sum them and you have the integral.

02 / WASHER AROUND X-AXIS

Washer method.

V = π ∫ (f(x)² − g(x)²) · dx

Slices the solid into thin disks or rings perpendicular to the x-axis. Outer radius f(x), inner radius g(x). Stack the slices like coins, sum the volumes.
